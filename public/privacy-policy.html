Effective Date: February 7, 2026

Your birth data is used solely for your personal reports. We do not sell or share your sensitive information with third-party advertisers. All communication is encrypted to ensure privacy.

4. Third-Party Services

We use secure payment gateways (like PhonePe) and Firebase for notifications. These partners have their own privacy policies governing your transaction data.
