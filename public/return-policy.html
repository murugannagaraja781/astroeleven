🌙 Return Policy

At Astro Eleven, we aim to provide you with the best astrology consultations and services. Since our services are primarily delivered digitally (calls, chats, and reports), returns are not applicable for consultations once delivered.

Physical Products

However, in rare cases where a product (like gemstone, yantra, or puja item) is purchased and delivered physically, returns will only be accepted if:

  • The product is damaged or defective upon delivery.
  • A wrong product was sent.

In such cases, the return request must be raised within 7 days of delivery, and the product must be unused and in its original packaging.
