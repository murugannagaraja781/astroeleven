🌙 Shipping Policy

Most of our services (consultations, reports) are delivered digitally, hence shipping is not applicable.

Physical Products

For physical products (e.g., gemstones, yantras, puja samagri):

  • Orders are usually processed within 3–5 business days.
  • Delivery timelines depend on location and courier partners.
  • Tracking details will be shared once the product is dispatched.
  • Shipping charges (if any) will be displayed at checkout.

Note: International shipping may involve customs duties and import charges, which must be borne by the customer.
